## 🔴 High / critical (all verified)

### Credibility & accuracy
- ✅ **USFWS Jan 2025 rule did NOT propose critical habitat.** The site claimed "proposed critical habitat … Clear Lake, Thurston Lake, and 18 tributaries" in 4+ spots (timeline 75/661, archive 896/899 + tag 904). The rule proposed *threatened status + a 4(d) rule*; critical habitat was explicitly deferred. Corrected all occurrences.
- ✅ **Hero overclaim.** Hero said "We **monitor, protect, and advocate**" — contradicting the About page's honest "compiles publicly available information / not a government agency" scope. Reworded to match.
- ✅ **Archive USFWS citation URL** pointed at the wrong Federal Register doc (Jan 22 / 2025-01502) while labeled "90 FR 4916 (Jan 16)". Repointed to the canonical doc the timeline uses.
- ✅ **"36 documented fish species" but the dataset has 35** (the "Show all 35 species" button contradicted the "36" text above it). Made the count derive from the data.
- ✅ **Sacramento Pikeminnow status conflict** — timeline says "functionally extirpated," Fish Guide showed it as present/thriving with no status. Aligned the Fish Guide entry to the sourced timeline.
- ✅ **"2020: best year in over a decade"** sat next to the 2023 record (2,548) on the same chart, and standardized surveys only began in 2013. Reworded.
- ✅ **OFR 2025-1018 mischaracterized** as a "2017–2022 comprehensive fish survey"; it's a hitch gillnet-methods report with data through 2023. Retitled (timeline + map data).
- ✅ **"WFD" typo** (non-existent agency) on two Adobe photo captions → correct agency name.
- ✅ **Homepage spawner counts labeled "observed"** (1,042 / 1,567) are CDFW all-programs *synthesis* figures; the archive's raw tributary observations are far lower (23 in 2024). Relabeled as lake-wide combined estimates with a note.
- ✅ **Per-creek "Survey History" tables** show lake-wide totals under a creek heading (e.g. Burns shows "1,672" for 2020, a year it hosted zero). Relabeled the column "Lake-wide spawners."
- ✅ **/creeks hero badge "9 Active Spawning Streams"** — 3 of the 9 are not active (Restore/Survey/Concern). Changed to "9 Priority Tributaries."
- ✅ **StatsBar ("36 / 4 / 20 species")** had no source while neighboring charts do. Added a source line (and fixed 36→35).
- ✅ **Footer disclaimer too broad** ("all data may contain inaccuracies") undercut the now well-sourced figures. Scoped it to map geometry vs cited data.

### Structure, UX & a11y
- ✅ **Footer missing/inconsistent.** The shared footer (nav + rescue line + disclaimer) rendered only on the homepage; /about, /faq, /archive, /timeline had no footer or no in-site nav. Added the shared footer to the content pages. (IMPROVEMENTS.md line 73's "footer nav across all pages = done" was inaccurate; corrected.)
- ✅ **/archive ended on developer docs** ("📝 How to add new reports", with a raw code template) shown to all visitors. Removed from the rendered page (kept as a code comment).
- ✅ **Forms accept empty submissions** and show "Thanks!" with no validation. Added required fields, aria-required, inline errors, and gated the success state. (Backend still intentionally deferred.)
- ✅ **Embedded mini-map unstyled chart.** The "96% decline" USGS gillnet bars used classes (`mbr/mbl/mbt/mbf/mbc`) with **no CSS** — the headline data story rendered as bare text. Added the styles.
- ✅ **/map had no `<h1>`/`<h2>`** (outline started at h3) on an indexed route. Added a visually-hidden h1 (not in embed mode).
- ✅ **Map panel contrast failures** (inactive tabs 2.5:1, placeholder 2.2:1, source links, data labels — all < 4.5:1 AA). Lightened the tokens.
- ✅ **Amber `#c87800` as small text** fails AA (3.1–3.4:1). Added a darker `--amber-text` token for text use.
- 🔧 **Embedded map iframe fixed at 520px** clips the stacked mobile layout. Making the height responsive.
- ⬜ **/creeks "Priority Tributaries" bar chart** shows invented pct bar lengths (95/90/…/30) with no axis/source — reads as fabricated precision. → relabel as a qualitative scale or drop the bar for status chips.
- 🅿️/⬜ **Interactive /map is a navigation dead-end** — clicking a creek opens an info panel but never links to that creek's profile (the throwaway homepage map does). → add "View full creek profile →" in the map's info panel.
- ⬜ **CRITICAL — Interactive /map is mouse/touch-only.** Tabs are non-focusable `<div>`s; creeks/zones are SVG elements with no tabindex/role/keydown; SVG is `role="img"`. Keyboard/AT users can't select anything. Now front-and-center since it's iframed on the homepage. → real tablist buttons + focusable, labeled, keyboard-operable markers.
- ✅ **"Become a Community Scientist" pushes off-site** to the county page instead of the project's own sighting form right below it. Made the form the destination + added a 3-step on-ramp (county link kept as secondary).
- ✅ **next.config `images: { unoptimized: true }`** disabled Vercel image optimization site-wide. Removed (no static export in use).
